xor 操作是二进制数字之间的操作，意思：xor 是Exclusive Or的缩写

^代表了二进制运算的xor操作。意思是if and only if has one 1.的时候才是1，否则为0.

结果： 1^2=3 1^3=2 2^3=1

This operation is performed between every two corresponding bits of a number.

z = y because x ^ y ^ x = y So it is entirely possible for a combination a ^ b = y = z. In fact, for every a there exists a b such that a ^ b = z. To calculate that, b = z ^ a. Be aware that XOR is commutative: this means that x ^ y = y ^ x.
